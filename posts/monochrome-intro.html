Monochrome is more or less a personal experiment/project and attempt to create a digital time machine for my future self. Oddly, I'm fascinated by monochrome (back and white) photography and film. I could probably watch The Twilight Zone all day if I didn't have the equivalent of a child's attention span. I will attempt to publish an indefinite amount of pictures per month. I must disclaim for the critics. I am not a professional or even an amateur photographer. I do, however, intend to learn/experiment/evolve during my journey. So without further ado, feel free to tag along.
